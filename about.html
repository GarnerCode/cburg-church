About Us

We are located at 308 McDaniel St in Callisburg, Texas. Each Sunday, we have Sunday School available for all ages starting at 9:30am, followed by our morning worship services starting at 10:30am. We later have our evening worship services starting at 6:00pm.

Annual Events

Bring your friends and family to our three-day-long Vacation Bible School, where kids learn about God and the bible through fun interactive activities such as reenactments of stories from the bible, arts and crafts, singing, and more!

Join in our annual scavenger hunt for canned foods! We split up into groups and travel Callisburg collecting can food donations from local residents and donate them to the food bank so that the less fortunate can enjoy fresh meals!

Take a relaxing trip out to Lake Texoma for our annual weekend-long Men's Fishing Trip! Come catch some fish with us and enjoy a fish fry at the end of the weekend!
